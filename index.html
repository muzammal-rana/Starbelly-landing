Introducing

Introducing Star Belly Dream Lites®, the soft, huggable, plush nightlight that makes bedtime fun! Star Belly Dream Lites® comforts kids who are afraid of the dark, so they can sleep soundly. Simply press the button on the belly to turn on the night light and it projects a starry sky onto the ceiling, instantly. The Mobile Motion™ mechanism rotates the star lights and will lull your child to sleep. With two speeds, you can choose from slow to fast. There are 6 different colored lights, so your child can pick their favorite color. Perfect for playtime and bedtime! Star Belly Dream Lites® comes in a variety of different plush friends to choose from and each with their own unique personality. Your little one will love sleeping and cuddling their new nighttime companion with Star Belly Dream Lites ®!
